About

Our SIG brings together people committed to structural change. We focus on practical methods and governance that embed justice into health data, services, and policy.

What we do

  • Host regular seminars and skill-shares
  • Curate resources and good practice
  • Support collaborative research and advocacy
  • Connect practitioners across sectors

How to get involved

  • Join our mailing list
  • Propose a talk or workshop
  • Start or join a working group
  • Contribute to our resources
